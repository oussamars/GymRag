Q: What are the gym opening hours?
A: The gym is open Monday through Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM. On Saturdays and Sundays, it is open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Q: What equipment is available?
A: The gym includes treadmills, stationary bikes, rowing machines, elliptical trainers, squat racks, cable machines, free weights, benches, resistance machines, and stretching areas.

Q: What group classes do you offer?
A: We offer Yoga, HIIT, Spinning, Strength Training, Pilates, Zumba, and Functional Fitness classes throughout the week.

Q: What time are Yoga classes?
A: Yoga classes are held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday at 7:00 PM.

Q: What time are HIIT classes?
A: HIIT classes are held every Tuesday and Thursday at 6:30 PM and every Saturday at 10:00 AM.

Q: Are there classes suitable for beginners?
A: Yes. Yoga, Pilates, and Beginner Strength Training are recommended for members who are new to fitness.

Q: Do I need to book classes in advance?
A: Yes. Members can reserve a class through the gym app or at the front desk up to 24 hours before the class starts.

Q: Is there a personal trainer available?
A: Yes. Certified personal trainers are available for one-on-one coaching by appointment.

Q: Do you provide lockers?
A: Yes. Free daily lockers are available for all members. Members must bring their own padlock.

Q: Are towels provided?
A: Premium members receive complimentary towels. Other members may rent a towel for 10 MAD per visit.

Q: Is parking available?
A: Yes. Members have access to free parking for up to three hours during their workout.

Q: Can I freeze my membership?
A: Yes. Memberships can be frozen for a minimum of one month and a maximum of three months per calendar year for medical or travel reasons.

Q: Can I cancel my membership?
A: Yes. Monthly memberships require a 30-day written notice before cancellation. Annual memberships may be cancelled according to the membership agreement.

Q: Can I bring a guest?
A: Yes. Members may bring one guest per visit by paying the guest pass fee. Premium members receive two free guest passes each month.

Q: What is the minimum age to join?
A: Members must be at least 16 years old. Members under 18 require written parental consent.

Q: Is there a dress code?
A: Members must wear clean athletic clothing and closed athletic shoes while using the gym.

Q: Are food and drinks allowed?
A: Water and sports drinks are allowed. Food is only permitted in the lounge area.

Q: What should I do if equipment is damaged?
A: Please report damaged equipment immediately to the reception desk. Do not continue using faulty equipment.

Q: Do you offer fitness assessments?
A: Yes. Premium and Elite members receive complimentary fitness assessments every three months.

Q: Do you offer student discounts?
A: Yes. Students with a valid student ID receive a 15% discount on monthly memberships.
