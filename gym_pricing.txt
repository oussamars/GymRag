# gym_pricing.txt

Basic Monthly Membership
Price: 300 MAD per month. Includes full gym access, locker room access, free parking, and access during all opening hours.

Premium Monthly Membership
Price: 450 MAD per month. Includes everything in the Basic plan, unlimited group classes, complimentary towels, one fitness assessment every three months, and two free guest passes each month.

Elite Monthly Membership
Price: 650 MAD per month. Includes everything in the Premium plan, four personal training sessions each month, priority class booking, unlimited guest passes, and one nutrition consultation every month.

Annual Membership
Price: 3,000 MAD per year. Includes full gym access, unlimited group classes, complimentary towels, two fitness assessments per year, and about two months free compared with paying monthly.

Registration Fee
New members pay a one-time registration fee of 150 MAD.

Student Membership
Price: 255 MAD per month. Requires a valid student ID and is available only to members under 26 years old.

Family Membership
Price: 800 MAD per month. Includes membership for up to three family members. Additional family members may be added for 150 MAD each per month.

Day Pass
Price: 80 MAD. Includes full gym access for one day and locker room access.

Guest Pass
Price: 50 MAD per visit. Premium members receive two free guest passes every month, and Elite members receive unlimited guest passes.

Personal Training
Single Session: 200 MAD. Pack of 10 Sessions: 1,800 MAD.

Towel Rental
Price: 10 MAD per visit. Free for Premium and Elite members.

Membership Freeze
Price: Free. Available for one to three months per calendar year for eligible members.
